The NFL Draft Game

Return to Game

In the first round of the NFL Draft, there are 32 selections. The objective of the game is to accurately assign the team and player for each of the 32 selections.
Each time you assign a team to the correct draft position, you will earn 1 point. If you assign a player to the correct team, you will earn 1 additional point. Thus, you can earn a maximum of 2 points per selection by correctly assigning the team and player.

If the correct team is selected, the team name and number will be highlighted in green. If the correct player is selected, the selection will be highlighted in green.
Contrarily, if selections are incorrect, the respective elements will be highlighted in red.
